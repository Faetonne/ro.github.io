22 DIY Water Fountain Ideas That Will Save You Major Time and Money

Add a soothing water structure to your backyard without breaking the bank.

By Madison Alcedo and Jenae Sitzes Apr 3, 2019 At Charlotte's House/A Piece of Rainbow

Want to add a unique element to your backyard ? Opt for a few one-of-a-kind DIY water fountains! If you're completely clueless as to how to make a water fountain in the first place, though, don't fret. Here you'll find our favorite DIY outdoor fountains, as well as beautiful options you can simply buy. From a DIY fountain made from flower pots to a homemade water fountain that rivals store-bought renditions, these easy and unique projects will reinvigorate your garden like nothing else. Plus, check out more of our best backyard decorating ideas that will be sure to wow your guests during summer soirées .
