fountain

noun Save Word foun·​tain | \ ˈfau̇n-tᵊn \

Definition of fountain

(Entry 1 of 2)

1 : the source from which something proceeds or is supplied 2 : a spring of water issuing from the earth 3 : an artificially produced jet of water also : the structure from which it rises 4 : a reservoir containing a liquid that can be drawn off as needed 5 : soda fountain sense 2

fountain

verb fountained ; fountaining ; fountains

Definition of fountain (Entry 2 of 2)

intransitive verb

: to flow or spout like a fountain

transitive verb

First Known Use of fountain

Noun

Verb

1903, in the meaning defined at intransitive sense

History and Etymology for fountain

Noun

Middle English, from Anglo-French funtaine, fontaine , from Late Latin fontana , from Latin, feminine of fontanus of a spring, from font-, fons
